"A minibike is a two wheeled, off-highway recreational vehicle popularized in the 1960s and 1970s, but available continuously from a wide variety of manufacturers since 1959. Traditionally, minibikes have a four-stroke, horizontal crankshaft engine, single or two-speed Centrifugal clutch transmission with chain final drive, 4" or 6" wheels and a low frame/seat height with elevated handlebars.
