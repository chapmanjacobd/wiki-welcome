"Coat of arms of the Bahamas

Armiger Elizabeth II in Right of the Bahamas

Adopted 7 December 1971

Crest Upon a representation of Our Royal Helmet mantled Azure doubled Argent on a Wreath Or and Azure. A Conch Shell proper in front of a Panache of Palm Fronds proper.

Torse Orange and Azure

Blazon Upon a representation of the Santa Maria on a base barry wavy of four Azure and Argent on a Chief Azure demisun Or.

Supporters On the dexter side a Marlin proper and on the sinister side a Flamingo
