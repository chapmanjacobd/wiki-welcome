La Niña de los Peines

Pastora Pavón Cruz, known as La Niña de los Peines (Seville, Spain, 10 February 1890 – 26 November 1969), is considered the most important woman flamenco singer of the 20th century. She was a sister of singers Arturo Pavón and Tomás Pavón, also an important flamenco singer, and aunt to Arturo Pavón, the first flamenco pianist.
