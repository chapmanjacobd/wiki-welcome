A turbine (/ˈtɜːrbaɪn/ or /ˈtɜːrbɪn/) (from the Latin turbo, a vortex, related to the Greek τύρβη, tyrbē, meaning "turbulence") is a rotary mechanical device that extracts energy from a fluid flow and converts it into useful work.
