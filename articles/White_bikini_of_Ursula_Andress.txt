White bikini of Ursula Andress

Designer Ursula Andress, Tessa Prendergast

Year 1962 (1962)

Type White cotton bikini

The white bikini of Ursula Andress (also known as the Dr. No bikini) was a white bikini worn by Ursula Andress as Honey Ryder in the 1962 James Bond film, Dr. No. It is cited as the most famous bikini of all time and an iconic moment in cinematic and fashion history.

Andress's white bikini is regarded as monumental in the history of the bikini, and sales of the two-piece bikin
