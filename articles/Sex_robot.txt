Sex robots or sexbots are hypothetical anthropomorphic robot sex dolls. As of 2018, although elaborately instrumented sex dolls have been created by a number of inventors, no fully functioning sex robots exist. In 2017, the "Harmony" sex doll was created, which can speak and make facial expressions.
