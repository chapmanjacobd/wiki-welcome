Tilted sedimentary bedding in shales of the Cretaceous Salto del Fraile Formation, Peru.

Beds are the layers of sedimentary rocks that are distinctly different from overlying and underlying subsequent beds of different sedimentary rocks. Layers of beds are called strata. They are formed from sedimentary rocks being deposited on the Earth's solid surface over a long periods of time.
