Scientific dating of the age of the Earth

The Blue Marble, Earth as seen from Apollo 17

The age of the Earth is estimated to be 4.54 ± 0.05 billion years (4.54 × 109 years ± 1%). This age may represent the age of the Earth's accretion, or core formation, or of the material from which the Earth formed.
