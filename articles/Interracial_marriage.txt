"Marriage between individuals of different racial/ethnic backgrounds

Richard and Mildred Loving, appellants in Loving v. Virginia, the landmark US Supreme Court case which struck down laws making interracial marriage illegal.

Interracial marriage is a form of marriage outside a specific social group (exogamy) involving spouses who belong to different races or racialized ethnicities.
