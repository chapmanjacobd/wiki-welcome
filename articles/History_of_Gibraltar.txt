"History of a peninsula on the Iberian coast

North View of Gibraltar from Spanish Lines by John Mace (1782)

Location of Gibraltar in the far south of the Iberian Peninsula

The history of Gibraltar, a small peninsula on the southern Iberian coast near the entrance of the Mediterranean Sea, spans over 2,900 years. The peninsula has evolved from a place of reverence in ancient times into "one of the most densely fortified and fought-over places in Europe", as one historian has put it. Gibraltar'
