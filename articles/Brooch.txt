A brooch /ˈbroʊtʃ/ or, in US English also /ˈbruːtʃ/, somewhat frequently broach (of which it is a variant), is a decorative jewelry item designed to be attached to garments, often to hold them closed. It is usually made of metal, often silver or gold or some other material.
