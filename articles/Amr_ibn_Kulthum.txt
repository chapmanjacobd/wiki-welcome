Amr ibn Kulthum Ibn Malik Ibn A`tab Abu Al-Aswad al-Taghlibi (Arabic: عمرو بن كلثوم‎) (died 584) was a poet and chieftan of the Taghlib tribe in pre-Islamic Arabia. One of his poems was included in the Mu'allaqat.
