The Lion of the Confederacy, or Lion of Atlanta in Oakland Cemetery carved by T. M. Brady in 1894 is thought of as a near copy of this monument.

The Lion Monument (German: Löwendenkmal), or the Lion of Lucerne, is a rock relief in Lucerne, Switzerland, designed by Bertel Thorvaldsen and hewn in 1820–21 by Lukas Ahorn. It commemorates the Swiss Guards who were massacred in 1792 during the French Revolutio
