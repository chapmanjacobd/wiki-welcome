Michael Ming-Chiao Lai (Chinese: 賴明詔; born 8 September 1942) is a Taiwanese virologist.

A native of Tainan born on 8 September 1942, Lai graduated from National Tainan First Senior High School and studied medicine at National Taiwan University. After completing his doctorate at the University of California, Berkeley in 1973, Lai joined the University of Southern California faculty, where he remained until 2003.
