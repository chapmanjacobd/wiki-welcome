This article is about confectionery in bar form. For other uses, see Candy bar (disambiguation).

A candy bar is a type of sugar confectionery that is in the shape of a bar. The most common type of candy bar is the chocolate bar, including both bars made of solid chocolate and bars combining chocolate with other ingredients, such as nuts, caramel, nougat, and wafers.
