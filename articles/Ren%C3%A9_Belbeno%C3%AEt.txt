Jules René Lucien Belbenoît (French pronunciation: ​; 4 April 1899 –25 February 1959) was a French prisoner on Devil's Island who successfully escaped to the United States. He later published the memoirs, Dry Guillotine (1938) and Hell on Trial (1940), about his exploits.
