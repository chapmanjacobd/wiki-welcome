The southwestern China

Southwest China (Chinese: 西南; pinyin: Xīnán; lit.: 'Westsouth') is a region of the People's Republic of China defined by governmental bureaus that includes the municipality of Chongqing, the provinces of Sichuan, Yunnan and Guizhou, and the Tibet Autonomous Region.
