Coat of arms of Senegal

Armiger Republic of Senegal

Adopted 1965

Crest Green star

Blazon Per pale, the first Gules a lion rampant Or, the second Or, a baobab-tree proper and in base a fess wavy Vert

Supporters Palm leaves

Motto Un Peuple, Un But, Une Foi

"One Nation , One Goal, One Faith"

Order(s) Star of the Ordre National du Lion
