Tayeb Salih

Born 12 July 1929

Al Dabbah, Sudan

Died 18 February 2009(2009-02-18) (aged 79)

London, England

Occupation Novelist, Columnist

Alma mater University of Khartoum, University of London

Literary movement Postcolonialism

Notable works Season of Migration to the North, The Wedding of Zein

Tayeb Salih (Arabic: الطيب صالح‎; 12 July 1929 – 18 February 2009) was one of Sudan's greatest authors of the twentieth century.
