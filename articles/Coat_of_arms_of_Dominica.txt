"Coat of arms of Dominica

Armiger Commonwealth of Dominica

Adopted 1961

Crest A Rocky Mount Sable thereon a Lion Passant guardant Or

Torse Argent and Azure

Blazon Quarterly Or and Azure a cross fillet counterchanged in the first quarter on a Rocky Mount Sable a Coconut Tree fructed proper in the second a Dominica Crapaud also proper in the third on Water Barry wavy in base a Carib canoe with sail set all likewise proper in the fourth quarter on a Rocky Mount also sable a Banana Tree fructed
