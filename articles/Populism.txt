
"Populist" redirects here. For other uses, see Populist (disambiguation).

Political philosophy that supports needs and desires of "the people" over those of "the powerful."

With its rhetoric of "the 99%" (the people) against "the 1%" (the elite), the international Occupy movement was an example of a populist social movement
