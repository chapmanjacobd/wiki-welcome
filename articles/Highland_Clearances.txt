Highland Clearances Ruined croft houses on Fuaigh Mòr in Loch Roag. The island was cleared of its inhabitants in 1841 and is now used only for grazing sheep.

Date Mostly 18th–19th centuries

Outcome Significant emigration of Highlanders to the coast, the Scottish Lowlands and further afield to North America and Australasia
