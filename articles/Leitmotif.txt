A leitmotif or leitmotiv (/ˌlaɪtmoʊˈtiːf/) is a "short, constantly recurring musical phrase" associated with a particular person, place, or idea. It is closely related to the musical concepts of idée fixe or motto-theme. The spelling leitmotif is an anglicization of the German Leitmotiv (IPA: ), literally meaning "leading motif", or "guiding motif".
