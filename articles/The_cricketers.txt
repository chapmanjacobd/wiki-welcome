"The cricketers

Artist Russell Drysdale

Year 1948

Medium oil on canvas

Dimensions 76 cm × 101.5 cm (30 in × 40.0 in)

Location Private collection

The cricketers is a 1948 painting by the Australian artist Russell Drysdale. The painting depicts three boys set among the buildings in an empty town; two playing cricket and the other watching them. The National Gallery of Australia describes the painting as "one of the most original and haunting images in all Australian art." The Sydney Mornin
