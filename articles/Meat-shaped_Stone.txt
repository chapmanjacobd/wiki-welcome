Artistic masterpiece from Qing China

The Meat-shaped Stone

Type Jasper sculpture

Dimensions H 5.73 cm, W 6.6 cm, D 5.3 cm 

Location National Palace Museum, Taipei

The Meat-shaped Stone (Chinese: 肉形石; pinyin: ròuxíngshí) is a piece of jasper carved into the shape of a Dongpo pork. It is part of the collection of the National Palace Museum in Taipei, Taiwan.
