Old Kingdom of Egypt

c. 2600 BC–c. 2100 BC

During the Old Kingdom of Egypt (circa 2686 BC – circa 2181 BC), Egypt consisted of the Nile River region south to Elephantine, as well as Sinai and the oases in the western desert.
