Tang dynasty tomb figure

Tang dynasty art (simplified Chinese: 唐朝艺术; traditional Chinese: 唐朝藝術) is Chinese art made during the Tang dynasty (618–907). The period saw great achievements in many forms—painting, sculpture, calligraphy, music, dance and literature.
