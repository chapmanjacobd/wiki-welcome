La Mort de Marat

Artist Jacques-Louis David

Year 1793

Medium Oil on canvas

Dimensions 162 cm × 128 cm (64 in × 50 in)

Location Royal Museums of Fine Arts of Belgium

The Death of Marat (French: La Mort de Marat or Marat Assassiné) is a 1793 painting by Jacques-Louis David of the murdered French revolutionary leader Jean-Paul Marat.
