Marginalized communities in the south Asian caste system

Dalit,(Sanskrit: दलित, romanized: dälit) meaning "broken/scattered" in Sanskrit and Hindi, is a term mostly used for the lower caste members in India. The term is used for those who have been subjected to untouchability.
