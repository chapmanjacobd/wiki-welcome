Héloïse (/ˈɛloʊ.iːz/ or /ˈhɛloʊ.iːz/; French: ; 1090?/1100–1? – 16 May 1164) was a French nun, writer, scholar, and abbess. Héloïse is accorded an important place in French literary history and in the development of feminist representation.
