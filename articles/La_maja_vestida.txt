"The Clothed Maja

Spanish: La maja vestida

Artist Francisco de Goya

Year 1800–1805

Medium Oil on canvas

Dimensions 97 cm × 190 cm (38 in × 75 in)

Location Museo del Prado, Madrid

The Clothed Maja (Spanish: La maja vestida ) is a pendant painting by the Spanish painter Francisco de Goya between 1800 and 1805. It is a clothed version of the earlier La maja desnuda (1797–1800) and is exhibited next to it in the same room at the Prado Museum in Madrid.
