Assorted sashimi: tuna, cuttlefish, and seabream

Sashimi (刺身, English: /səˈʃiːmi/ sə-SHEE-mee, Japanese: ) is a Japanese delicacy consisting of fresh raw fish or meat sliced into thin pieces and often eaten with soy sauce.
