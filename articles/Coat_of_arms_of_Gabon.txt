"Coat of arms of Gabon

Armiger Gabonese Republic

Adopted 15 July 1963

Blazon Or, a ship sable, masts of the same, with the flag of Gabon, tierced in fess vert, or and azure, sailing upon a sea azure; a chief vert charged with three bezants.

Supporters Two black panthers

Compartment Okoumé tree

Motto Union, Travail, Justice

"Unity, Work, Justice"
