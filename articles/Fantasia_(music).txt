Musical interpretation rooted in improvisation

For other uses, see Fantasia (disambiguation).

See also: Category:Fantasias (music).

A fantasia (Italian: ; also English: fantasy, fancy, fantazy, phantasy, German: Fantasie, Phantasie, French: fantaisie) is a musical composition with its roots in the art of improvisation. Because of this, the fantasia, like the impromptu, seldom follows the textbook rules of any strict musical form.
