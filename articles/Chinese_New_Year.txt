Chinese New Year

Fireworks over the Victoria Harbour in Hong Kong; fireworks and firecrackers are a traditional element of Chinese New Year celebrations.

Also called Spring Festival, Lunar New Year

Observed by Chinese people, Sinophone communities and non-Chinese people worldwide
