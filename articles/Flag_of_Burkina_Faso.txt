Use National flag

Proportion 2:3

Adopted 4 August 1984

Design Two horizontal bands of red and green with a yellow five-pointed star in the center.

The national flag of Burkina Faso (French: drapeau du Burkina Faso) is formed by two equal horizontal bands of red (top) and green, with a yellow five-pointed star resting in the center.
