Davis Sea as part of the Southern Ocean,with proposed names for bordering seas also depicted.

Davis Sea is an area of the sea along the coast of East Antarctica between West Ice Shelf in the west and the Shackleton Ice Shelf in the east, or between 82° and 96°E.
