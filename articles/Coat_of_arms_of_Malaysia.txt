Coat of arms of Malaysia

Jata Negara Malaysia

Armiger The Yang di-Pertuan Agong

Adopted 1965

Crest Yellow crescent and yellow fourteen-pointed federal star

Blazon Equal divisions of four topped by a single row of five krises, with a Pinang palm and the Penang Bridge to the left, a Malacca tree to the right and divisions containing the arms of Sabah and of Sarawak and a hibiscus below

Supporters Two tigers rampant

Motto Bersekutu Bertambah Mutu (Malay)

'Unity is Strength'
