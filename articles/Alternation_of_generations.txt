"Reproductive cycle of plants and algae

This article describes alternation of generations in plants; for a similar phenomenon in animals see Heterogamy

Diagram showing the alternation of generations between a diploid sporophyte (bottom) and a haploid gametophyte (top)

Alternation of generations (also known as metagenesis) is the type of life cycle that occurs in those plants and algae in the Archaeplastida and the Heterokontophyta that have distinct haploid sexual and diploid asexual stages.
