"This article is about the animal part used as food. For the type of Japanese rice dish, see Tendon (Japanese cuisine).

The tendons of certain animals (particularly beef tendon) are used as an ingredient in some Asian cuisines, including the Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Indonesian, Thai and Vietnamese traditions.
