A dollhouse or doll's house is a toy home made in miniature. For the last century dollhouses have primarily been the domain of children, but their collection and crafting is also a hobby for many adults. North-American English-speakers commonly use the term dollhouse. In the UK (United Kingdom) the term is doll's house (alternatively: dolls' house).
