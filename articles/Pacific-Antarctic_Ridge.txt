The Pacific-Antarctic Ridge is the southern extension of the East Pacific Rise.

The Pacific-Antarctic Ridge (PAR) is a divergent tectonic plate boundary located on the seafloor of the South Pacific Ocean, separating the Pacific Plate from the Antarctic Plate.
