Sujeo, a set of Korean eating utensils.

Korean name

Hangul 수저

Revised Romanization Sujeo

McCune–Reischauer Sujŏ

Sujeo (수저) is the Korean word for the set of eating utensils commonly used to eat Korean cuisine. The word is a portmanteau of the words sutgarak (숟가락, "spoon") and jeotgarak (젓가락, "chopsticks").
