"Overview of television in South Korea

This article is about television in South Korea. For the article about Korean televised dramas, see Korean drama.

In South Korea, there are a number of national television networks, the three largest of which are KBS, MBC, and SBS. Most of the major television studios are located on Yeouido and Sangam-dong.
