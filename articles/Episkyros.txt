Episkyros (Ancient Greek: Ἐπίσκυρος, Epískyros; also Ἐπίκοινος, Epíkoinos, 'commonball') was an ancient Greek ball game. Highly teamwork oriented, the game was played between two teams of usually 12 to 14 players ea
