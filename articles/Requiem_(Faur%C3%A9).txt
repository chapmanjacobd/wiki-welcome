Page from the manuscript of the Requiem: In paradisum, m. 413, Bibliothèque Nationale, Paris

Gabriel Fauré composed his Requiem in D minor, Op. 48, between 1887 and 1890. The choral-orchestral setting of the shortened Catholic Mass for the Dead in Latin is the best-known of his large works.
