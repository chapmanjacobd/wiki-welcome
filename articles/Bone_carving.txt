"Bone carving encompasses the acts of creating art, tools, and other goods by carving animal bones, antlers, and horns. It can result in the ornamentation of a bone or the creation of a distinct object. It has been practiced by a variety of world cultures, sometimes as a cheaper, and recently a legal, substitute for ivory carving.
