Bas-relief plaque of Tribonian in the Chamber of the United States House of Representatives in the United States Capitol.

Tribonian (Greek: Τριβωνιανός , c. 485?–542) was a notable Byzantine jurist and advisor, who during the reign of the Emperor Justinian I, supervised the revision of the legal code of the Byzantine Empire.
