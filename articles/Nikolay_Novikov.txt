Nikolay Ivanovich Novikov (Russian: Никола́й Ива́нович Новико́в) (8 May  1744, Moscow Governorate – 12 August  1818. Moscow Governorate) was a Russian writer and philanthropist most representative of his country's Enlightenment. Frequently considered to be the first Russian journalist, he aimed at advancing the cultural and educational level of the Russian public.
