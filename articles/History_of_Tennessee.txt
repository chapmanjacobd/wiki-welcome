The Tennessee State Capitol in Nashville

Tennessee is one of the 50 states of the United States. It was admitted to the Union on June 1, 1796, as the 16th state. Tennessee was initially part of North Carolina, and later part of the Southwest Territory. Tennessee was the last state to leave the Union and join the Confederacy at the outbreak of the American Civil War in 1861.
