A hatchback is a car with a hatch-type rear door that often opens upwards and often a shared volume for the passenger and cargo areas.

When the body style of a car is described as a hatchback, typically it refers to a utilitarian small car (especially in the U.S.); however hatchbacks are also used on sports cars, SUVs, and large luxury cars. Compact hatchbacks are very popular
