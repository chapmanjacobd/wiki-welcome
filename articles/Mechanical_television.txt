Televisor" redirects here. It is not to be confused with a television set, called a "televisor" in several languages.

Watching a homemade mechanical-scan television receiver in 1928. The "televisor" (right) which produces the picture uses a spinning metal disk with a series of holes in it, called a Nipkow disk, in front of a neon lamp.
