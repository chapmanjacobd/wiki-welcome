Pyotr Chaadayev

Pyotr or Petr Yakovlevich Chaadayev (Russian: Пётр Я́ковлевич Чаада́ев; also spelled Chaadaev, or Čaadajev; June 7 , 1794 – April 26 , 1856) was a Russian philosopher. He was one of the Russian Schellingians.
