"This article is about the history of the scientific concept of energy. For the history of how humans use energy, see Energy.

The word energy derives from Greek ἐνέργεια (energeia), which appears for the first time in the 4th century BCE works of Aristotle (OUP V, 240, 1991) (including Physics, Metaphysics, Nicomachean Ethicsand De Anima).
