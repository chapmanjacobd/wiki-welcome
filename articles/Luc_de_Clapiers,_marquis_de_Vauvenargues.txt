Luc de Clapiers, marquis de Vauvenargues

Luc de Clapiers, marquis de Vauvenargues (French: ; 6 August 1715 – 28 May 1747) was a French writer and moralist. He died at age 31, in broken health, having published the year prior—anonymously—a collection of essays and aphorisms with the encouragement of Voltaire, his friend.
