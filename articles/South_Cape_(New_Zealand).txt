South Cape, at 47°17′24″S 167°32′16″E / 47.29000°S 167.53778°E / -47.29000; 167.53778, is a Cape on the south coast of Stewart Island, New Zealand. It is the southernmost point on Stewart Island. South Cape is one of the 4 New Zealand Cardinal Capes named by James Cook, Commander of the Endeavour in the 1769-70 voyage.
