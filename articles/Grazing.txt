"feeding livestock on forage

This article is about a method of feeding in animal husbandry. For herbivory in animal behaviour, see grazing (behaviour). For the human eating pattern, see grazing (human eating pattern).

A Maasai herdsman grazing his cattle inside the Ngorongoro crater.

In agriculture, grazing is a method of animal husbandry whereby domestic livestock are used to convert grass and other forage into meat, milk, wool and other products, often on land unsuitable for arable farming.
