The Ballad of Sexual Dependency (1986). The image on the cover is "Nan and Brian in Bed" (1981).

The Ballad of Sexual Dependency is a 1985 slide show exhibition and 1986 artist's book publication of photographs taken between 1979 and 1986 by photographer Nan Goldin.
