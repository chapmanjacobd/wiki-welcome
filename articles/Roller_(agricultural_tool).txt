"This article is about non-powered (agricultural) rollers. For self-propelled rollers, see road roller.

A roller in a typical power farming application

The roller is an agricultural tool used for flattening land or breaking up large clumps of soil, especially after ploughing or disc harrowing. Typically, rollers are pulled by tractors or, prior to mechanisation, a team of animals such as horses or oxen.
