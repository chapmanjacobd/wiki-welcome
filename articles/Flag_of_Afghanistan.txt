"National flag

Islamic Republic of Afghanistan

Use State and war flag, state and naval ensign

Proportion 2:3

Adopted August 19, 2013

Design Three vertical bands of black, red and green with the National Emblem in white centered on the red band and then slightly overlapping the black and green bands.

The national flag of Afghanistan (Dari Persian: پرچم افغانستان, Pashto: افغانستان توغ) consists of a vertical tricolor with the classical National Emblem in the center.
