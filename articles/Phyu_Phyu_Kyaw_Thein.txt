Phyu Phyu Kyaw Thein (Burmese: ဖြူဖြူကျော်သိန်း) is a prominent Burmese pop singer, known for her stage presence and costumes, which has drawn comparisons to Lady Gaga. She has been a UNICEF celebrity ambassador since 2008.
