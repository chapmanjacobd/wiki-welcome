Portrait of a bearded man (poet Archilochos?). Roman copy c. 2nd century BCE of the Greek original of 4 century BCE.

Coin from ancient Thasos showing Satyr and nymph, dated to late 5th century BCE.

Archilochus was involved in the Parian colonization of Thasos about two centuries before the coin was minted. His poetry includes vivid accounts of life as a warrior, seafarer and lover.
