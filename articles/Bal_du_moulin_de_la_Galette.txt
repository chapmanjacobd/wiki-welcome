"Bal du moulin de la Galette

Artist Pierre-Auguste Renoir

Year 1876

Medium Oil on canvas

Dimensions 131 cm × 175 cm (52 in × 69 in)

Location Musée d'Orsay, Paris

Bal du moulin de la Galette (commonly known as Dance at Le moulin de la Galette) is an 1876 painting by French artist Pierre-Auguste Renoir. It is housed at the Musée d'Orsay in Paris and is one of Impressionism's most celebrated masterpieces. The painting depicts a typical Sunday afternoon at the original Moulin de la Galette
