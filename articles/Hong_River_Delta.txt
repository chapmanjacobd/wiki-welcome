Location of the Hong River Delta region in Vietnam

The Hong River Delta or Red River Delta (Vietnamese: Đồng Bằng Sông Hồng, or Châu Thổ Sông Hồng) is the flat low-lying plain formed by the Red River and its distributaries merging with the Thai Binh River in northern Vietnam.
