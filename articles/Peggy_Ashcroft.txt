English actress (1907–1991)

Ashcroft in 1936

Dame Edith Margaret Emily Ashcroft, DBE (22 December 1907 – 14 June 1991) was an English actress whose career spanned more than 60 years.

Born to a comfortable middle-class family, Ashcroft was determined from an early age to become an actress, despite parental opposition.
