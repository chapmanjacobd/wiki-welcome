"Point at which water emerges from an aquifer to the surface

On an average day nearly 303 million US gallons (1,150,000 m3) of water flow from Big Spring in Missouri at a rate of 469 cubic feet per second (13.3 m3/s).

A spring is a point at which water flows from an aquifer to the Earth's surface. It is a component of the hydrosphere.
