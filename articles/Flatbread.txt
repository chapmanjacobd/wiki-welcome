Flatbread Homemade flatbread

Type Bread

Main ingredients Flour, water, salt

Cookbook: Flatbread

Media: Flatbread

A flatbread is a bread made with flour, water, and salt, and then thoroughly rolled into flattened dough. Many flatbreads are unleavened, although some are leavened, such as pita bread.

Flatbreads range from below one millimeter to a few centimeters thick so that they can be easily eaten without being sliced. They can be baked in an oven, fried in hot oil, grille
