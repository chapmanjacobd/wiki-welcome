Chino cloth (/ˈtʃiːnoʊ/ CHEE-noh) is a twill fabric, originally made of 100% cotton. The most common items made from it, trousers, are widely called chinos (or khakis if they're khaki-colored). Today it is also found in cotton-synthetic blends.
