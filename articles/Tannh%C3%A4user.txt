Tannhäuser (German: ; Middle High German: Tanhûser) was a German Minnesinger and poet. Historically, his biography is obscure beyond the poetry, which dates between 1245 and 1265. 

His name becomes associated with a "fairy queen"-type folk ballad in German folklore of the 16th century.
