"Caves near the village of Han-sur-Lesse, Belgium

The Caves of Han-sur-Lesse (in French: Grottes de Han-sur-Lesse or simply Grottes de Han) are a major Belgian tourist attraction (around 250-300,000 visitors per year) located on the outskirts of the village of Han-sur-Lesse.
