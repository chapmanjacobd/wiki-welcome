Coat of arms of East Timor

Armiger Democratic Republic of Timor-Leste

Adopted 2007

Blazon On the centre of the coat of arms, there a curved pyramid with red edges and black core, symbolise Mount Ramelau, the highest peak in Timor Leste. On the black field in the centre there is a five-pointed star with five rays of light. Under it there is an open red book rest upon a yellow industrial gear.
