Roman bronze reduction of Myron's Discobolus, 2nd century AD (Glyptothek, Munich)

3D model of a replica at Statens Museum for Kunst, Denmark.

The Discobolus of Myron ("discus thrower", Greek: Δισκοβόλος, Diskobólos) is a Greek sculpture completed at the start of the Classical Period, figuring a youthful ancient Greek athlete throwing discus, about 460–450 BC.
