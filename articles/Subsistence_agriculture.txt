"Farming which meets the basic needs of the farmer and family

A Bakweri farmer working on his taro field on the slopes of Mount Cameroon (2005).

Subsistence agriculture occurs when farmers grow food crops to meet the needs of themselves and their families. In subsistence agriculture, farm output is targeted to survival and is mostly for local requirements with little or no surplus.
