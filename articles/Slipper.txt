Slippers

Slippers on a shelf

Slippers are light footwear that are easy to put on and off and are intended to be worn indoors, particularly at home.

History

The recorded history of slippers can be traced back to the 12th century when the Vietnamese had been wearing slippers. But in the West, the record can only be traced to 1478.
