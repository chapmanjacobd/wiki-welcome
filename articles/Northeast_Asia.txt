Northeast Asia, North-East Asia or Northeastern Asia, is a geographical subregion of Asia; its northeastern landmass and islands, by the Pacific Ocean. 

The term Northeast Asia was popularized during the 1930s by American historian and political scientist Robert Kerner. Under Kerner's definition, "Northeast Asia" included the Mongolian Plateau, the Northeast China Plain
