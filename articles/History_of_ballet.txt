A publicity photo for the premiere of Tchaikovsky's ballet The Sleeping Beauty (1890).

Ballet is a formalized form of dance with its origins in the Italian Renaissance courts of 15th and 16th centuries. Ballet spread from Italy to France with the help of Catherine de' Medici, where ballet developed even further under her aristocratic influence.
