A Russian Nagant M1895 revolver

Russian roulette (Russian: русская рулетка, russkaya ruletka) is a highly lethal game of chance in which a player places a single round in a revolver, spins the cylinder, places the muzzle against their head, and pulls the trigger in hopes that the loaded chamber doesn't align with the primer percussion mechanism and the barrel, causing the weapon to discharge.
