Pasha Angelina

Pasha Angelina's tractor depicted on the coat of arms of the Starobesheve Raion of Donetsk oblast

Praskovia Nikitichna Angelina (Russian: Праско́вья Ники́тична Анге́лина), popularly known as Pasha Angelina (Russian: Па́ша Анге́лина) (December 30  1912 – January 21, 1959) was a celebrated Soviet udarnik and Stakhanovite at the time of the first Five Year Plans. She was glorified as one of the first female tractor-operators in the USSR
