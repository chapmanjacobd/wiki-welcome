Group of three islands on the west coast of Ireland

The Aran Islands (Irish: Oileáin Árann—pronunciation: ) or The Arans (na hÁrainneacha—) are a group of three islands located at the mouth of Galway Bay, on the west coast of Ireland, with a total area of about 46 km2 (18 sq mi). They constitute the barony of Aran in County Galway, Ireland.
