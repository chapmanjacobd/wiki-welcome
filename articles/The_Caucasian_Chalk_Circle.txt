"The Caucasian Chalk Circle

Written by Bertolt Brecht

Date premiered 1948

Place premiered Carleton College, Minnesota, US

Original language German (premiered in English)

Subject Parenthood, property, war

Genre Epic theatre

Setting Georgia

The Caucasian Chalk Circle (German: Der kaukasische Kreidekreis) is a play by the German modernist playwright Bertolt Brecht. An example of Brecht's epic theatre, the play is a parable about a peasant girl who rescues a baby and becomes a better mother
