"The Farm

Catalan: La Masia

Artist Joan Miró

Year 1921–1922

Medium Oil on canvas

Dimensions 123.8 cm × 141.3 cm (48.7 in × 55.6 in)

Location National Gallery of Art, Washington, D.C.

The Farm is an oil painting made by Joan Miró between the summer of 1921 in Mont-roig del Camp and winter 1922 in Paris.
