Malawi

Use National flag, civil and state ensign

Proportion 2:3

Adopted 6 July 1964 (re-adopted in 2012)

Design A horizontal triband of black, red, and green; charged with a red rising sun with 31 rays centred on the black stripe

The flag of Malawi (Chichewa: mbendera ya Malaŵi) was officially adopted on 6 July 1964 when the colony of Nyasaland became independent from British rule and renamed itself Malawi.
