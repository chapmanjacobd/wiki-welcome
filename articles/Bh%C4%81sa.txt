"Bhasa" redirects here. For the word for "language", see Bahasa. For other uses, see Bhasa (disambiguation).

Bhāsa is one of the earliest and most celebrated Indian playwrights in Sanskrit, predating Kalidasa. His plays had been lost for centuries until the manuscripts were rediscovered in the early 20th century.
