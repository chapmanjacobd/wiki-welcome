This is a Chinese name; the family name is Bi.

Bì Shēng (990–1051 AD) was a Chinese artisan and inventor of the world's first movable type technology, with printing being one of the Four Great Inventions of Ancient China. Bi Sheng's system was made of Chinese porcelain and was invented between 1039 and 104
