"At the Moulin Rouge, The Dance

Artist Henri de Toulouse-Lautrec

Year 1890

Medium Oil on canvas

Dimensions 115.6 cm × 149.9 cm (45.51 in × 59.02 in)

Location Philadelphia Museum of Art

At the Moulin Rouge, the Dance is an oil-on-canvas painted by French artist Henri de Toulouse-Lautrec. It was painted in 1890, and is the second of a number of graphic paintings by Toulouse-Lautrec depicting the Moulin Rouge cabaret built in Paris in 1889.
