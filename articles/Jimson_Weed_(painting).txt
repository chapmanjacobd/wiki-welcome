"Jimson Weed

Artist Georgia O'Keeffe

Year 1936

Type Oil on linen

Dimensions 180 cm × 212 cm (70 in × 83.5 in)

Location Indianapolis Museum of Art, Indianapolis

 Jimson Weed is an oil on linen painting by American artist Georgia O'Keeffe from 1936, located in the Indianapolis Museum of Art in Indianapolis, Indiana. It depicts four large blossoms of jimson weed. A similar work by O'Keeffe, Jimson Weed/White Flower No. 1, was sold by the Georgia O'Keeffe Museum at auction to Walmart heiress
