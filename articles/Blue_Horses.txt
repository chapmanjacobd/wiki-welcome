Blue Horses

Artist Franz Marc

Year 1911

Medium Oil on canvas

Dimensions 105.7 cm × 181.2 cm (​41 5⁄8 in × ​71 5⁄16 in)

Location Walker Art Center, Minneapolis, Minnesota

Owner Walker Art Center, Gift of the T.B. Walker Foundation and the Gilbert M. Walker Memorial Fund, 1942.1

Blue Horses or Die grossen blauen Pferde (The Large Blue Horses) is a 1911 painting by German painter and printmaker Franz Marc (1880–1916).
