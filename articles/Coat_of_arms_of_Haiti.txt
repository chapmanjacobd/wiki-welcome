The coat of arms of Haiti was originally introduced in 1807, and has appeared in its current form since 1986.

It has many flags which shows six draped flags of the country, three on each side, which are located before a palm tree and cannons on a green lawn. On the lawn various items are found, such as a drum, bugles, long guns, and ship anchors. Above
