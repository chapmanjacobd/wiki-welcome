A symbolic portrayal of human evolution, wrongly implying that evolution is linear and progressive.

"Missing link" is an unscientific term for a transitional fossil. It is often used in popular science and in the media for any new transitional form. The term originated to describe the hypothetical intermediate form in the evolutionary series of anthropoid ancestors to anatomically modern humans (hominization).
