Vasily Kirillovich Trediakovsky (Russian: Васи́лий Кири́ллович Тредиако́вский; 5 March  1703 in Astrakhan – 17 August  1769 in Saint Petersburg) was a Russian poet, essayist and playwright who helped lay the foundations of classical Russian literature.
