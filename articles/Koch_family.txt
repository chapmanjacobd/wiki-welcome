The Koch family (/ko äk/ KOHK) is an American family engaged in business, best known for their political activities (donating to libertarian, criminal justice reform, and Republican Party causes) and their control of Koch Industries, the second-largest privately owned company in the United States (with 2017 revenues of $100 billion).
