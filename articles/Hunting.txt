Searching, pursuing, catching and killing wild animals

Hunter on the stand during a driven hunt in Finland

Hunting is the practice of seeking, pursuing and capturing or killing wild animals. Hunting wildlife or feral animals is most commonly done by humans for meat, recreation, to remove predators that can be dangerous to humans or domestic animals, to remove pests
