Konstantin Ushinsky

Konstantin Dmitrievich Ushinsky (Russian: Константи́н Дми́триевич Уши́нский; Ukrainian: Костянтин Дмитрович Ушинський) (2 March  1823 – 3 January 1871 ) was a Russian teacher and writer, credited as the founder of scientific pedagogy in Russia.
