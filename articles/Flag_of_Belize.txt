Belize

Use Civil and state flag

Proportion 3:5

Adopted 21 September 1981 (standardized on 28 August 2019)

Design A royal blue field with two red narrow horizontal stripes along the top and the bottom edges and the large white disk in the center bearing the National Coat of Arms.

The flag of Belize was adopted on 21 September 1981, the day Belize became independent. It consists of the Coat of Arms on a blue field with red stripes at the top and bottom.
