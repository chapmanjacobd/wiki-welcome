photograph of Nazi atrocities in Ukraine

Einsatzgruppen murdering Jewish civilians in Ivanhorod, Ukraine, 1942.

The Ivanhorod Einsatzgruppen photograph is an image of the Holocaust, showing a soldier aiming a rifle at a woman who is trying to shield a child with her body. It depicts the murder of Jews by an Einsatzgruppen death squad near Ivanhorod, Ukraine, in 1942.
