Symphony No. 9

by Anton Bruckner

Key D minor

Catalogue WAB 109

Composed 1887 (1887) – 1896 (1896): (unfinished)

Dedication To the beloved God

Movements 4 started, 3 completed

Symphony No. 9 in D minor is the last symphony on which Anton Bruckner worked, leaving the last movement incomplete at the time of his death in 1896; Bruckner dedicated it "to the beloved God" (in German, dem lieben Gott). The symphony was premiered under Ferdinand Löwe in Vienna in 1903.
