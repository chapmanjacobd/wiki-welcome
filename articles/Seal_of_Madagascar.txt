"Seal of Madagascar

Armiger Republic of Madagascar

Adopted circa 1998

Motto Tanindrazana, Fahafahana, Fandrosoana

"Homeland, Liberty, Development"

The seal of Madagascar (French: Sceau de Madagascar) includes an outline map of the island at the center (together with two smaller islands nearby-the Glorioso Islands and Tromelin Island), and below it the head of a zebu.
