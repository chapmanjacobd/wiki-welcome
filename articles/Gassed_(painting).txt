"Gassed

Artist John Singer Sargent

Year c. March 1919

Type Oil

Dimensions 231 cm × 611 cm (91 in × 240½ in)

Location Imperial War Museum, Lambeth Road, London, England

Gassed is a very large oil painting completed in March 1919 by John Singer Sargent. It depicts the aftermath of a mustard gas attack during the First World War, with a line of wounded soldiers walking towards a dressing station.
