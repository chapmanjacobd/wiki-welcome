A clockwise spinning low pressure area or cyclone off southern Australia. The center of the spiral-shaped cloud system is also the center of a low, and usually is where the pressure is lowest.

This low-pressure system over Iceland spins counter-clockwise due to balance between the Coriolis force and the pressure gradient force.
