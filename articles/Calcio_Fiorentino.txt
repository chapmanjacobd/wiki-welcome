Calcio match in Piazza Santa Maria Novella in Florence. Painting by Jan Van der Straet

Calcio fiorentino (also known as calcio storico "historic football") is an early form of football (soccer and rugby) that originated in 16th-century Italy. Once widely played, the sport is thought to have started in the Piazza Santa Croce in Florence.
