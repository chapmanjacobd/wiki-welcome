Ohshikohchi Mitsune by Kikuchi Yōsai

In this Japanese name, the family name is Ōshikōchi.

Ōshikōchi no Mitsune (凡河内 躬恒) was an early Heian administrator and waka poet of the Japanese court (859–925), and a member of the Thirty-six Poetry Immortals.
