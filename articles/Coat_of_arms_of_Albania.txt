Coat of arms of Albania

Armiger Republic of Albania

Adopted 2002: standardized by

Law #8926, 22 July 2002

The coat of arms of Albania (Albanian: Stema e Shqipërisë) is an adaptation of the flag of Albania. It is based on the flag and seal of Gjergj Kastriot Skanderbeg. The emblem above the head of the two-headed eagle is the helmet of Skanderbeg, surmounted with billy goats' horns.
