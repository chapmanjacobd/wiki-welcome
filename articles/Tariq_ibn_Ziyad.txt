Ṭāriq ibn Ziyād (Arabic: طارق بن زياد‎) also known simply as Tarik in English, was a Berber Umayyad lieutenant who under command of Musa ibn Nusayr was sent to lead the Muslim conquest of Visigothic Hispania (Present day Spain and Portugal) in 711–718
