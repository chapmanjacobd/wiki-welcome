An undershirt in AmE, (also vest in BrE& IndE), is an article of underwear worn underneath a dress shirt intended to protect it from body sweat and odors. It can have short sleeves (T-shirt) or be sleeveless (A-shirt). The term most commonly refers to upper-body innerwear worn by males.
