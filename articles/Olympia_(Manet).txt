Olympia

Artist Édouard Manet

Year 1863

Medium Oil on canvas

Dimensions 130.5 cm × 190 cm (51.4 in × 74.8 in)

Location Musée d'Orsay, Paris

Olympia is a painting by Édouard Manet, first exhibited at the 1865 Paris Salon, which shows a nude woman ("Olympia") lying on a bed being brought flowers by a servant.
