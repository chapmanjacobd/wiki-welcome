Applejohn Applejack

A bottle of blended apple brandy, along with a Jack Rose, a cocktail made with applejack.

Traditionally produced Applejohn Blue Label Blend, Applejohn Granny Smith

Applejack is a strong apple-flavored alcoholic drink produced from apples. Popular in the American colonial era, the drink's prevalence declined in the 19th and 20th centuries amid competition from other spirits. 

Applejack is used in several cocktails, including the Jack Rose.
