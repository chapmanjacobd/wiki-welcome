Ripe hazelnuts

Corylus colurna, Turkey

A sweeper gathers hazelnuts in an orchard.

The hazelnut is the nut of the hazel and therefore includes any of the nuts deriving from species of the genus Corylus, especially the nuts of the species Corylus avellana.
