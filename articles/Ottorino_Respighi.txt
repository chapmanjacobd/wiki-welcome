"Italian composer, musicologist and conductor

Ottorino Respighi

Ottorino Respighi (/rɛˈspiːɡi/ reh-SPEE-ghee, also US: /rəˈ-/ rə-, Italian: ; 9 July 1879 – 18 April 1936) was an Italian composer, violinist, and teacher who was one of the leading Italian composers of the early 20th century.
