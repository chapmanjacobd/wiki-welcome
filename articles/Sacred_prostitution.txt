Inanna/Ishtar, Mesopotamian goddess of sex and fertility, depicted on a ceremonial vase.

Sacred prostitution, temple prostitution, cult prostitution, and religious prostitution are general terms for a sexual rite consisting of sexual intercourse or other sexual activity performed in the context of religious worship, perhaps as a form of fertility rite or divine marriage (hieros gamos).
