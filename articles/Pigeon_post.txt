Young lady in oriental clothing with a homing pigeon (19th century painting)

Pigeon post (1843 painting by Miklós Barabás)

Pigeon post is the use of homing pigeons to carry messages. Pigeons were effective as messengers due to their natural homing abilities. The pigeons were transported to a destination in cages, where they would be attached with messages, then the pigeon would naturally fly back to its home
