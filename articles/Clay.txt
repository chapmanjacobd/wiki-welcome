A finely-grained natural rock or soil containing mainly clay minerals

Gay Head cliffs in Martha's Vineyard consist almost entirely of clay.

Clay is a type of fine-grained natural soil material that contains hydrous aluminium phyllosilicates (clay minerals) that develops plasticity when wet.
