"Triptych of the Temptation of St. Anthony

Artist Hieronymus Bosch

Medium Oil on panel

Dimensions 131 cm × 228 cm (52 in × 90 in)

Location Museu Nacional de Arte Antiga, Lisbon

The Triptych of Temptation of St. Anthony is an oil painting on wood panels by the Early Netherlandish painter Hieronymus Bosch, dating from around 1501. The work tells the story of the mental and spiritual torments endured by Saint Anthony the Great (Anthony Abbot), one of the most prominent of the Desert Fathers
