Paul Cézanne: The Card Players 1894–1895, Musée d'Orsay, Paris

The Card Players is a series of oil paintings by the French Post-Impressionist artist Paul Cézanne. Painted during Cézanne's final period in the early 1890s, there are five paintings in the series.
