The transport of pipe segments for the Great Manmade River in the Sahara desert, Libya, during the 1980s: a network of pipes that supplies water from the Nubian Sandstone Aquifer System, a fossil aquifer in the Sahara Desert of Libya. The Great Manmade River is the world's largest irrigation project.
