Huda Sha'rawi

Huda Sha'arawi (Arabic: هدى شعراوي‎, ALA-LC: Hudá Sha‘rāwī; June 23, 1879 – December 12, 1947) was a pioneering Egyptian feminist leader, suffragette, nationalist, and founder of the Egyptian Feminist Union.
