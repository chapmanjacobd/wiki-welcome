"Guerrillero Heroico

Alberto Korda's photograph of Che Guevara

Artist Alberto Korda

Completion date March 5, 1960 (1960-03-05)

Type Photograph

The original image, from which the popularized portrait was derived. By cropping out a palm tree and the profile of another man, and making other slight adjustments, Korda gave Guevara's image "an ageless quality, divorced from the specifics of time and place."
