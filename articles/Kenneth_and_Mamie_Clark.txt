Dr. Kenneth B. Clark (Chicago Urban League Records, University of Illinois at Chicago Library)

Kenneth Bancroft Clark (July 14, 1914 – May 1, 2005) and Mamie Phipps Clark (April 18, 1917 – August 11, 1983) were African-American psychologists who as a married team conducted research among children and were active in the Civil Rights Movement.
