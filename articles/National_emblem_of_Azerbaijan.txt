The state emblem of Azerbaijan (Azerbaijani: Azərbaycan gerbi) mixes traditional and modern symbols. The focal point of the emblem is a stylized flame, a reference to Azerbaijan's many natural everlasting gas fires, which has given it the nickname "land of eternal fire," as well as a historic reference to Zoroastrianism.
