Location of Cocos (Keeling) Islands at 12°07′S 96°54′E / 12.117°S 96.900°E / -12.117; 96.900 in the Indian Ocean.

Captain William Keeling (1577 – 19 September 1619), of the East India Company, was a British sea captain. He commanded the Susanna on the second East India Company voyage in 1604.
