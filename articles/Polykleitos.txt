Polykleitos was an ancient Greek sculptor in bronze of the 5th century BC. His Greek name was traditionally Latinized Polycletus, but is also transliterated Polycleitus (Ancient Greek: Πολύκλειτος, Classical Greek Greek pronunciation: , "much-renowned") and due to iotacism in the transition from Ancient to Modern Greek, Polyklitos or Polyclitus.
