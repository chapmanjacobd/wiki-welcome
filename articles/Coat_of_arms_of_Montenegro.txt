"Coat of arms of Montenegro

Armiger Montenegro

Adopted 12 July 2004

The coat of arms of Montenegro (Montenegrin: grb Crne Gore / грб Црне Горе) was officially adopted by the law passed in the Parliament on 12 July 2004. It is now the central motif of the flag of Montenegro, as well as the coat of arms of the Army of Montenegro.
It was constitutionally sanctioned by the Constitution proclaimed on 2 October 2007.
