Nikolai Gumilyov during his senior years in gymnasium

Nikolay Stepanovich Gumilyov (Russian: Никола́й Степа́нович Гумилёв, IPA:  (listen); April 15 NS 1886 – August 26, 1921) was an influential Russian poet, literary critic, traveler, and military officer. He was a cofounder of the Acmeist movement. He was husband of Anna Akhmatova and father of Lev Gumilev. Nikolay Gumilev was arrested and executed by the Cheka, the secret Soviet police force, in 1921.
