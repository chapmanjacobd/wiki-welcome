"This article is about the historical region of Eastern Arabia. For the present-day state, see Bahrain.

Eastern Arabia (historical region of Bahrain) on a 1745 Bellin map

Eastern Arabia was historically known as Al-Bahrain (Arabic: اَلْبَحْرَيْنِ‎) until the 18th century. This region stretched from the south of Basra along the Persian Gulf coast and included the regions of Bahrain, Kuwait, al-Hasa, Qatif, United Arab Emirates, Qatar, Southern Iraq, Ahwaz and Northern Oman.
