Set of metabolic pathways that breaks down molecules into smaller units; metabolism is usually divided into two categories: catabolism (the breaking down of organic matter) and anabolism

Schematical diagram

Catabolism (/kəˈtæbəlɪsm/) is the set of metabolic pathways that breaks down molecules into smaller units that are either oxidized to release energy or used in other anabolic reactions.
