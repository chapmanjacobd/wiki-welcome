"Rabbit

Artist Jeff Koons

Year 1986 (1986)

Medium Stainless steel sculpture

Subject Rabbit

Rabbit is a 1986 stainless steel sculpture by Jeff Koons. It is the most expensive work sold by a living artist at auction, being sold for $91.1 million in May 2019.
