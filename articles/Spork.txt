Four types of sporks

A spork is a hybrid form of cutlery taking the form of a spoon-like shallow scoop with two to four fork-like tines.  Spork-like utensils, such as the terrapin fork or ice cream fork, have been manufactured since the late 19th century; patents for spork-like designs date back to at least 1874.
