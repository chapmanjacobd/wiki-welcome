Olga Bergholz in 1930

Olga Fyodorovna Bergholz (Russian: Ольга Фёдоровна Берггольц, IPA:  (listen); May 16  1910 – November 13, 1975) was a Soviet poet, writer, playwright and journalist. She is most famous for her work on the Leningrad radio during the city's blockade, when she became the symbol of city's strength and determination.
