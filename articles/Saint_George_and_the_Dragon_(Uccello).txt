"Saint George and the Dragon

Artist Paolo Uccello

Year c. 1470

Medium Oil on canvas

Dimensions 55.6 cm × 74.2 cm (21.9 in × 29.2 in)

Saint George and the Dragon is a painting by Paolo Uccello dating from around 1470. It is on display in the National Gallery, London, United Kingdom.
