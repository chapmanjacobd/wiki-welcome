La maja desnuda, Francisco Goya, c. 1797–1800. Museo del Prado, Madrid. 97cm × 190cm

The Nude Maja (Spanish: La Maja Desnuda ) is a name given to a c. 1797–1800 oil on canvas painting by the Spanish artist Francisco Goya. It portrays a nude woman reclining on a bed of pillows, and was probably commissioned by Manuel de Godoy, to hang in his private collection in a separate cabinet reserved for nude paintings.
